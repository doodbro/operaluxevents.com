About

Derek
Rue Lyric Tenor

Derek Rue is a professional opera singer and lyric tenor based in Trier, Germany, with fifteen years of stage experience performing across Germany and Europe. A graduate of the Universität Mozarteum Salzburg, Derek brings the full power of the operatic tradition into modern venues with warmth, presence, and a genuine connection with his audience.

From intimate birthday celebrations to corporate galas, retirement parties, cultural events, and embassy functions, Derek delivers a performance that is powerful, personal, and completely unforgettable.
