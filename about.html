Derek Rue · Lyric Tenor

Derek Rue is a professional opera singer and lyric tenor based in Trier, Germany, with fifteen years of stage experience performing across Germany and Europe. A graduate of the Universität Mozarteum Salzburg — one of the world's foremost conservatoires — Derek brings the full power and artistry of the operatic tradition into modern venues with warmth, presence, and a genuine connection with his audience.

His voice combines the brilliance and agility of the lyric tenor with a rich, warm core that carries equally well in an intimate restaurant dining room and a grand concert hall. Every performance is shaped by the room, the occasion, and the people in it.

From intimate birthday celebrations and retirement milestones to corporate galas, cultural events, and embassy functions, Derek delivers something guests talk about long after the evening ends — a performance that is powerful, personal, and completely unforgettable.

Based in the Moselle wine region of Germany, Derek performs regularly across Germany and throughout Europe, bringing world-class opera directly to events of every scale and character.
