Derek Rue · Principal Singer, Theater Trier

Derek grew up in Pasadena, California, trained at the Universität Mozarteum Salzburg, and sang his early engagements at the Salzburg Festival. Today he is a principal singer at Theater Trier, with fifteen years on stage across Germany and Europe.

Somewhere along the way he noticed what happens when a performer with an opera voice steps outside the opera house. In a restaurant or a hotel ballroom there's no orchestra pit, no distance. People put down their forks. Conversations stop mid-sentence. And the guests who react most are the ones who'd never call themselves opera fans. That moment is why Opera Lux exists.

Every programme is planned personally with the host: which songs, when in the evening, how much the room can take. On the night Derek reads the guests and adjusts as he goes. A dinner for twenty and a gala for four hundred need very different evenings, and both get one built for them.

When you book Opera Lux, you're not booking an agency roster. You're booking Derek himself. He's based in the Moselle wine region and performs regularly across Germany and Luxembourg, and throughout Europe.

What Derek Brings

Presence & Warmth

Derek watches the room and adjusts as the evening moves: softer while people talk, full voice when everyone leans in. Guests get greeted between sets, sung to at their tables, included in the evening.

A Working Opera Singer

Derek sings professionally year-round: lead roles at Theater Trier, concerts, and private events. He can fill a concert hall, or sing a few metres from your guests without overwhelming them. Live, every time.

Flexible Repertoire

Nessun Dorma next to Bring Him Home next to a Lehár waltz. Derek builds each programme with the host, around the occasion and the crowd.

One Singer, No Fuss

No band, no long soundcheck, no stage build. Derek arrives with full equipment and professional orchestral backing, and the room stays yours.
